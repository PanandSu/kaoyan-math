$$
\int\kern{-8pt}\int \kern{-23mu} \bigcirc ( x^2,y^2,z^2 ) ds=4\pi ( a^2,b^2,c^2 ) R^2+\frac{3\pi R^4}{4}
$$

$$
\int\kern{-8pt}\int \kern{-23mu} \bigcirc ( xy,yz,xz ) ds=4\pi ( ab,bc,ac ) R^2
$$

$$
